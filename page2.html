3. How to stop this Pollution?

We can only stop this pollution if everyone of us decides to limit the use of plastic and following the principle of 3 R's

  • REDUCE

    The concept of reducing what is produced and what is consumed is essential to the waste hierarchy. The logic behind it is simple to understand - if there is less waste, then there is less to recycle or reuse.

  • REUSE

    You may either reuse the items like waste paper,old jeans or t-shirts or any clothes,newspaper,old jars and pots, electronic items,etc. for your own use or donate or sell these items to us so that others can use them.

  • RECYCLE

    The last stage of the waste hierarchy is to recycle. To recycle something means that it will be transformed again into a raw material that can be shaped into a new item.
